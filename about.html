Plans Are Nothing, Planning Is Everything!!!

A Successful Marriage requires falling in Love and we create that first atmosphere for the couple and their families to cherish the memories forever. Our Role Begins At The Conceptual Stage And Does Not End Until The Parents Sleep At Ease After The Bidai. We Plan, Design And Execute The Wedding As If, It's One In Our Family. We Believe In Delivery.

Intricately designed stages in large, palatial gardens, rituals by a breezy seashore, hosting hundreds, even thousands, of guests in international locations is the Big Fat Indian Wedding. We dig deeper into the planning of such weddings and make it the celebration of a lifetime. From the monumental wedding stages and reception decor to the caterers offering every cuisine under the sun and the trousseau of the bride and groom, Indian weddings are never too conscious about putting up a show. Our photographers bring to the board more creativity for a candid documentation of the big day.
